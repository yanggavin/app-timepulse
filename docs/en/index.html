TimePulse support

Help for the TimePulse habit tracker, flip clock, mood log, and optional iCloud sync.

Contact

For questions or feedback, email support@timepulse-app.newtree.tech.

Common topics

  • iCloud sync (TimePulse Pro) — Turn on iCloud for your device in Settings, then enable sync inside TimePulse. Data syncs through your Apple ID and CloudKit; we do not host your habit or mood content on our servers.
  • Subscriptions — Purchases are processed by Apple. Manage or cancel in the App Store app under your Apple ID → Subscriptions, or on your device in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.
  • Restore purchases — Open TimePulse → Settings and use the subscription / restore options shown there (Customer Center or system restore, depending on your build).
  • Data on this device — You can clear local data from TimePulse settings. If iCloud sync is on, clearing may affect other devices signed into the same iCloud account—read the in-app confirmation carefully.
