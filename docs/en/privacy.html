Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026. Describes how TimePulse (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use the mobile app.

1. What TimePulse is

TimePulse is an iOS app for habits, a flip-style clock display, mood logging, and optional insights.

2. Data stored on your device

Habits, completion history, mood entries, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple’s on-device frameworks (including SwiftData). This data is under your control and is used to provide app functionality.

3. iCloud (optional, Pro)

If you enable iCloud sync and your build supports it, selected app data may sync through Apple’s CloudKit service tied to your Apple ID. Apple processes that data under Apple’s privacy policy and iCloud terms. We do not receive the contents of your habit or mood database on our own servers for that sync path.

4. Purchases and subscriptions

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. Apple provides us with limited transaction-related information needed to validate entitlements (for example, through RevenueCat or similar tooling configured in the app). We do not receive your full payment card number from Apple.

5. Analytics and advertising

TimePulse is not designed to show third-party ads. Unless you add separate analytics SDKs in a custom build, we do not intentionally collect behavioral analytics across other companies’ platforms for sale. If that changes, we will update this policy and App Store privacy labels.

6. Crash and diagnostic data

Apple may collect crash diagnostics if you opt in to sharing analytics with developers via your device settings. We may use that information to fix bugs.

7. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your choices

  • Disable iCloud sync in TimePulse settings if you do not want CloudKit sync.
  • Delete app data using in-app controls, subject to iCloud behavior described in the app.
  • Remove the App from your device to delete local data (iCloud copies may remain until removed per Apple’s behavior).

9. International users

Apple operates infrastructure in multiple regions. Your use of the App Store and iCloud is subject to Apple’s regional practices.

10. Contact

Questions: support@timepulse-app.newtree.tech
