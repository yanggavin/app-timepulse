1. What TimePulse does

TimePulse is an iPhone app for viewing time, tracking daily habits, logging mood, and unlocking optional Pro features such as deeper insights and iCloud sync.

2. Data stored on your device

Habits, completion records, mood entries, preferences, and related app state are stored on your device using Apple frameworks, including SwiftData and UserDefaults.

3. iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync in a Pro-enabled build, selected app data may sync through Apple CloudKit under your Apple ID. We do not run our own cloud database for that sync path.

4. Purchases and subscriptions

Payments are processed by Apple. TimePulse may use RevenueCat to validate entitlement status and help manage subscriptions, but payment credentials are not provided to us.

5. Advertising, tracking, and analytics

TimePulse is not designed to serve third-party ads. The app does not intentionally track you across third-party apps or websites for advertising purposes.

6. Diagnostics

Apple may provide crash or diagnostic information if you choose to share analytics with developers in your device settings.

7. Your controls

  • Disable iCloud sync in app settings.
  • Clear local or synced data using the in-app settings flow.
  • Delete the app to remove its local data from the device.
